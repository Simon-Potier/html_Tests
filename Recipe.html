Melted dryed chocolates

This is going to be my favorite recipe

List of ingredients :

  • Dark chocolate
  • White chocolate

Steps :

  1. Break all the chocolate into small pieces
  2. Put all of it into a casserole on a feu doux
  3. Make it melt gently stirring
  4. when it is melt, put it out of the fire
  5. Let it dry (be patient)
  6. Enjoy !
  7. Enjoy again
